PERMA Police is for entertainment purposes only!


PERMA stands for Positive emotion, Engagement, Positive Relationships, Meaning, and Accomplishment. These are the five components of psychological wellbeing. Each has a negative counterpart: negative emotion, disengagement, negative relationships, lack of meaning, and lack of accomplishment.


PERMA Police scans each element on a page and assigns it a wellbeing rating based on the PERMA components above. If 3/5 negative components score higher than their positive counterparts the element will be redacted or the page will be blocked depending on what action you select below.
